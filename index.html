Introduction

Eastern Top Companys is a 100% Saudi Arabia Company Registered with ministry and chamber of commerce Commercial Reg. No: 7051448806 .

We are specialized in the field of industrial and commercial constructions: civil, mechanical, electro mechanical, HVAC System.

We are handling all types of turnkey projects.

The company is controlled by management team with a clear focus on individual responsibility and high level of expertise.

Qualified and experienced persons manage estimation, engineering management, planning constructions, safety quality control, quality assurance and cost control of projects.
