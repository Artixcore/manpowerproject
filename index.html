Introduction

About Us

At EASTERN TOP COMPANYS, we take pride in being a pioneer in industrial and commercial construction in Saudi Arabia.

Since 1970, we have been a leading contracting company, delivering high-quality, reliable, and innovative construction solutions. Our expertise spans across various industrial and commercial sectors, ensuring excellence in every project we undertake.

We are committed to providing our customers with quality, cost-effective and reliable solutions as an EPC contractor at home and manufacturer of plants and equipment in the kingdom.
